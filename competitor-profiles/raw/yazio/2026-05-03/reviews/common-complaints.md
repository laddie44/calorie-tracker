## Common Complaint Themes (2026)

### Ads in free version
- "Excessive ads in the free version"
- Forces users to upgrade to PRO for usable experience

### Database issues
- Items not found that exist in MyFitnessPal database
- Many duplicate entries for same product
- Users have to manually verify accuracy

### Recent UI/UX changes
- "Less convenient" after recent updates
- Multiple screens to filter through to log meals
- "3-4 unnecessary worthless animations pop up each time they add meals"
- Some users switching apps because logging takes too long

### Fasting feature limitations
- No simple start/stop button
- Have to select a timeframe in advance — bad for irregular schedules

### Fitness tracker integration
- Reconnection issues common

### Calorie tracking accuracy
- Only tracks active calories burned, not total — can lead to over-deficit

### Intrusive feedback
- "Helpful" recommendations after logging cannot be permanently turned off
- Constantly nitpicks even objectively good food choices

## Praise Themes
- Long, thorough onboarding feels personalized (~80 questions)
- Beautiful UI/visual design
- Good for fasting trackers and recipes
- Good progress bar UX
- Strong European following

## Onboarding Note
Per UserOnboarding.Academy and reviewers: ~80-question onboarding. Builds investment via long quiz. Cognitive bias-based design (Zalán Bernáth Medium analysis).
