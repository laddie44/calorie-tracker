## Common Complaint Themes

### Hidden / dynamic pricing — biggest complaint
- Pricing not visible until user completes full onboarding
- Different users see different prices (variable pricing model)
- "Fake discount pages" / "confusing billing"
- Reddit complaints about being charged without clear trial period
- "The Cal AI pricing isn't set in stone. It changes from person to person"

### Apple App Store violation (April 2026) — CRITICAL CONTEXT
- Briefly pulled from App Store
- Reasons: paywall showed weekly-calculated price more prominently than actual billed amount; toggle obscured automatic renewal
- Reinstated after fixing paywall

### Accuracy issues
- Simple single-item foods: 10–15% error margin (acceptable)
- Complex meals: 30–50% error common
  - Example: mixed salad estimated at 450 cal, actual ~800–900
- Hidden ingredients (cooking oil, sugar, dressings) consistently undercounted
- Sandwich content unclear → AI guesses
- Portion sizes often wrong
- Reddit r/loseit / r/caloriecount: "calories always undercounted"

### Onboarding paywall friction
- Hard paywall — must enter credit card before using app
- 28-step onboarding
- Forces 3-day trial → yearly auto-conversion
- Cannot try app at all without payment info

## Praise Themes
- Speed (snap and log in seconds)
- Photo logging UX is genuinely novel
- Modern AI-first design
- Streaks/milestones gamification
- Clean single-purpose UI
