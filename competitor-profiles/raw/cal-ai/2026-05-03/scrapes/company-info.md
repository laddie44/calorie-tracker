## Founding
- Founded: 2024 by Zach Yadegari (CEO), Henry Langmack (CTO), Blake W Anderson, Jake Castillo
- Founders met on Twitter/X and at coding camp

## Headquarters
Roslyn, NY (United States)

## App Launch
May 2024

## Scale (as of late 2024 / early 2025)
- 5M+ downloads in 8 months
- Reportedly $1.4M gross profit/month
- Founders claim 90% accuracy rate (founder claim, not independently verified)

## CRITICAL — Acquired by MyFitnessPal
- **March 2, 2026:** MyFitnessPal acquired Cal AI
- Cal AI's photo recognition tech is being integrated into MFP

## CRITICAL — App Store Compliance Issues
- April 2026: Briefly pulled from App Store by Apple for deceptive billing practices
- Issue: paywall displayed weekly-calculated price prominently while obscuring true subscription billing
- Reinstated after fixing the paywall

## Funding
Pre-acquisition: bootstrapped + small angel/VC round (per CNBC)

## Notable
Founders were teenagers (18–19) when company started. CEO Zach Yadegari named to Forbes 30 Under 30.
