## Common Complaint Themes (2026)

### April 2026 redesign backlash
- "Today tab" redesign making basic tasks require more taps
- Food diary buried behind "View All" button
- Per-meal macro breakdowns harder to find
- Users organizing 1-star review campaigns to pressure reversal

### Subscription/paywall complaints
- Features moved from free to paid (esp. barcode scanning)
- App crashes on free tier
- Intrusive ads on free plan
- "Returned after a break, can't barcode scan unless I upgrade"

### Billing/customer support
- BBB complaint: "Continues to charge credit card despite numerous attempts to cancel for more than 5 years"
- "No way to find a person at MyFitnessPal to talk to"

### Data accuracy
- ±6.8% calorie accuracy from user-submitted entries (per third-party review)
- Database has many duplicate entries with different values
- Users switching to Cronometer for accuracy

## Praise Themes
- Largest food database (20M+ items)
- Slick mobile UI (despite recent redesign issues)
- Mainstream familiarity
- 3.5M 5-star ratings across stores
