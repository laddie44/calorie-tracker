## Founding
- Founded: 2005 by Mike Lee and Albert Lee
- Crowdsourced food database

## Ownership History
- 2015: Acquired by Under Armour for ~$475M
- Dec 2020: Sold to Francisco Partners (private equity) for $345M
- Currently owned by Francisco Partners

## Recent (2025–2026) Strategic Moves — IMPORTANT
- 2025: Acquired Intent (meal planning startup) — features integrated into Premium+
- January 2026: Launched ChatGPT Health integration
- **March 2026: Acquired Cal AI** (the viral photo calorie app) — combining DB + AI photo recognition

## Scale
- 200M+ users (claimed on homepage)
- 80M+ users by 2015 acquisition
- Featured in: Multiple major fitness/health publications

## Headquarters
San Francisco, CA (per Wikipedia)
