## Common Complaint Themes (2026)

### Accuracy concerns
- ~±6.5% deviation from lab measurements (per third-party review)
- "Multiple entries for same product with different nutritional information"
- "Sometimes all the nutritional information is out of whack on two or more listings"
- Database verification is inconsistent

### Technical/stability issues (April 2026)
- "App has been getting worse with every update"
- Major stability issues
- Some users: app logs them out every reopen
- Recently logged items disappear

### Features/customization
- "Limited free tier" cited as main switching reason
- Mobile-only design (no robust web version)

## Praise Themes
- "Most visually appealing interface of any calorie tracker tested"
- Beautiful design language
- Easy meal logging
- Generally accurate barcode scanner
- Diet-specific meal plans (keto, Mediterranean) appreciated
- Supportive tone

## Overall Rating
- ~4.0–4.5 stars across app stores
- Trustpilot mixed; many positive but recurring stability complaints in 2026
