Frequently asked questions

MyFitnessPal and similar apps make you search a food database, choose serving sizes, and log meals manually. TextCalio works by text. You message Calio what you ate in plain English, and Calio logs your calories and macros in seconds — no app to open, no database to search.
No app, ever. TextCalio works entirely over SMS. Save Calio's number in your contacts and text like you would a friend. Your dashboard is a website that works on any phone.
For restaurant meals, Calio searches the restaurant's actual website in real time — not a static database. For packaged food, send a photo of the nutrition label and Calio reads the exact manufacturer values. For home-cooked meals, Calio uses verified USDA data as anchors.
Cancel anytime at support@textcalio.com or by texting Calio. If you cancel during your trial, you won't be charged at all. After that, cancellation takes effect at the end of your billing period.
Your data is only used to power Calio's tracking features. We never sell your information or use it for advertising. You can delete your entire account and all data by texting "delete account" to Calio at any time.
