# TextCalio — 30-Day Faceless Social Content Plan

*TikTok · Instagram Reels · YouTube Shorts*

*Last updated: 2026-04-29*

---

## Overview

Every video in this plan is:
- Faceless — no talking head, no face cam
- Made from screen recordings, text overlays, and free stock B-roll
- Under 30 seconds in most cases
- Producible from a phone or laptop with free tools
- Reusable across TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts

Posting pace: **2–3 videos per week** for 10–12 weeks. The 30 ideas below are batched into a calendar showing which week each belongs to and which 5 to make first.

## Rules for Staying Compliant

Follow these on every video, every caption, and every comment reply.

1. **No weight loss promises.** Say "hit your nutrition goals" or "reach your targets" — never "lose X lbs" or "get shredded."
2. **No medical claims.** TextCalio is not a medical service, dietitian, or clinical tool. Never say it "treats," "prevents," or "improves" any health condition.
3. **No before/after body transformation content.** Even implied body changes ("look better in 30 days") are a compliance risk.
4. **No shame or guilt language.** Never imply a user "failed" or "cheated." No "bad food," "cheat day," "guilty pleasure."
5. **No calorie restriction framing.** Say "your daily calorie target" not "your calorie limit."
6. **No accuracy guarantees.** Say "estimates" or "AI-powered estimate." Never "exact" or "precise" macros.
7. **Results language:** Use "help you" and "make it easier to" — never "will" or "guarantees."
8. **Endorsements:** If you ever pay or gift a creator, disclose it per FTC/ASC guidelines (#ad or #sponsored).

## Weekly Production Workflow (under 3 hours)

This is the repeatable system. Run it once a week to stay 1 week ahead.

### Monday — Plan (20 min)
- Pick the 3 videos for the current week from the calendar
- Confirm which ones are screen-recording-only vs. need B-roll
- For B-roll videos: search Pexels, download 2–3 clips, done

### Tuesday or Wednesday — Record (60–75 min)
- Record all screen content in one session (phone or laptop screen recorder)
- Batch record: do all SMS conversations in one sitting, even if they're for different videos
- For each demo: set up the real conversation with Calio so it's authentic, not staged

### Thursday — Edit (60 min)
- Import all clips into CapCut
- Add auto-captions (CapCut's built-in tool handles this in 30 seconds per video)
- Add text overlays, adjust timing
- Export all 3 videos

### Friday — Write and schedule (30 min)
- Copy caption template from this doc, personalize lightly
- Schedule in Buffer or Later for the following week's posting days
- Done

**Total: ~3 hours per week, 3 videos per week, 10 weeks of content**

## Screen Recording Notes

**On iPhone:**
- Add Screen Recording to Control Center (Settings → Control Center)
- Swipe down, tap record, wait 3-second countdown
- Shows a red bar at the top while recording
- Crop in CapCut to remove the recording indicator if needed

**Tip:** Film real interactions with Calio, not scripted demos. The slight delay and natural typing look more authentic than a pre-staged replay.

**For the split-screen comparison videos:** Record both sessions separately. Import both into CapCut → use the split-screen template. Speed up the MFP side to 1.5x; keep TextCalio at 1x. The contrast hits harder.
