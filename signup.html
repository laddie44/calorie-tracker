How were these numbers calculated? +
Calio uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation — the gold standard formula used by registered dietitians. It factors in your height, weight, age, and sex to calculate your basal metabolic rate, then adjusts for your activity level and goal (±500 cal for fat loss/gain). Macro splits are optimized for your specific goal.
