How were these numbers calculated? +
Calio uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation — a widely used formula. It factors in your height, weight, age, and sex to calculate your basal metabolic rate, then adjusts for your activity level and goal. Standard pace is ±500 cal for fat loss and +300 cal for gain; if you chose a different pace, that adjusts your calories accordingly. Macro splits are tuned for your specific goal.
