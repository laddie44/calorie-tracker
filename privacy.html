The short version: Calio collects your phone number, basic body stats, and food logs to provide personalized nutrition tracking. We don't sell your data. You can delete everything at any time by texting "delete account" to Calio.

Section 01

Information we collect

TextCalio Inc. ("TextCalio", "we", "us", or "our") operates Calio, an AI-powered nutrition tracking service available via SMS. We collect information in the following ways:

Information you provide directly

  • Phone number — collected when you first text Calio. This is your account identifier.
  • Name — your first name, collected during onboarding.
  • Body statistics — gender, age, weight, height, and activity level, collected during onboarding to calculate your calorie and macro targets.
  • Nutrition goal — your selected goal (fat loss, muscle gain, maintenance, or body recomposition).
  • Food logs — every meal, food item, or photo you send to Calio, including the estimated calories, protein, carbohydrates, and fat for each entry.
  • Weight logs — body weight entries you text to Calio over time.
  • Unit preference — whether you prefer metric or imperial measurements.
  • Macro tracking preferences — which macronutrients you choose to display and track.

Information collected automatically

  • Message history — the content of SMS conversations between you and Calio, stored to provide conversational context and memory across sessions.
  • Usage data — timestamps of messages, streak data, and last log date.

Information from third parties

  • Twilio — our SMS provider delivers your messages to us. Twilio may collect metadata about message delivery per their own privacy policy.
Section 02

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Calculate and display personalized daily calorie and macro targets using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation
  • Log and track your food intake, calorie totals, and macro breakdowns
  • Provide AI-powered food estimation and nutrition lookups via OpenAI
  • Send you your personalized daily SMS summary of your nutrition progress
  • Display your progress on your personal dashboard at textcalio.com
  • Maintain conversational memory so Calio can understand follow-up messages in context
  • Track your weight trend over time
  • Calculate and display your logging streak
  • Improve the accuracy and performance of the service
  • Send service-related communications (not marketing) when necessary

We do not use your health data for advertising purposes. TextCalio does not serve ads and does not allow advertisers to target you based on your nutrition or health data.

Section 03

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with the following third-party service providers, and only to the extent necessary to operate Calio:

  • Supabase — our database provider, hosted on AWS in the United States. Your account data, food logs, and weight logs are stored here.
  • OpenAI — we send your food descriptions and photos to OpenAI's API to estimate nutrition information. OpenAI may use API inputs to improve their models per their usage policies. We do not send your name or phone number to OpenAI.
  • Twilio — our SMS infrastructure provider. Twilio processes your phone number and message content to deliver SMS messages.
  • Vercel — our hosting provider, which runs the Calio web dashboard and API.

All third-party providers are contractually required to protect your information and may only use it to provide services to us.

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of TextCalio, our users, or the public.

Section 04

Data storage and security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers located in the United States. If you are located in Canada or another country, your data is transferred to and processed in the United States.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit
  • Cryptographic verification of all incoming SMS messages (Twilio signature validation)
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Service role key authentication for all database access
  • Token-based dashboard access — your dashboard URL cannot be guessed

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your information using industry-standard practices.

Conversation history older than 90 days is automatically deleted from our systems to limit data retention.

Section 05

Your rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to access

You can view all your food logs, weight logs, and targets at any time through your personal dashboard. You can also text "stats" or "my targets" to Calio for a summary.

Right to correction

You can correct any food log by texting "edit last [correction]" to Calio, or by using the edit button on your dashboard. You can update your targets anytime by texting "update goals."

Right to deletion

You can permanently delete your entire account and all associated data at any time by:

  • Texting "delete account" to Calio, then confirming with "confirm delete"

Deletion is permanent and irreversible. All your data — food logs, weight logs, conversation history, and account information — will be removed from our systems within 24 hours.

Right to opt out of communications

You can opt out of all SMS communications at any time by texting STOP to Calio. You can re-subscribe at any time by texting START.

Canadian users — PIPEDA

If you are located in Canada, you have additional rights under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), including the right to access your personal information and challenge its accuracy. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@textcalio.com.

Section 06

SMS communications

By completing the Calio onboarding process, you expressly consent to receive recurring automated SMS messages from TextCalio, including:

  • Responses to your food logging messages
  • Daily nutrition summary messages (if you have logged food that day)
  • Account-related service notifications

Message frequency varies based on your usage. Standard message and data rates from your carrier may apply.

To stop receiving messages, text STOP at any time. For help, text HELP or email support@textcalio.com.

TextCalio is not responsible for any delays or failures in receiving SMS messages due to carrier outages or network issues outside our control.

Section 07

Children's privacy

Calio is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 16, please contact us immediately at privacy@textcalio.com and we will delete the information promptly.

Section 08

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify active users via SMS at least 7 days before changes take effect.

Your continued use of Calio after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
