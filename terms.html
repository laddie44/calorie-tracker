AI-generated content

Calio uses artificial intelligence — specifically OpenAI's language and vision models — to estimate nutrition information, respond to messages, and analyze food photos and nutrition labels.

AI-generated nutrition estimates are approximations. They may be inaccurate, particularly for:

  • Home-cooked meals with multiple ingredients
  • Foods with variable preparation methods
  • Restaurant meals at unfamiliar establishments
  • Obscure or regional foods with limited training data

We continuously work to improve accuracy but cannot guarantee that any specific calorie or macro estimate is correct. You should use Calio's estimates as helpful approximations, not precise measurements.

Calio is an AI assistant, not a human. If you ask Calio directly, it will tell you it is an AI. Do not share sensitive personal, financial, or medical information with Calio beyond what is necessary for nutrition tracking.
