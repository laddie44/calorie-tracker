Why it happens

Why people quit MyFitnessPal

It's not a willpower problem. It's a friction problem. Here's what actually causes people to stop:

📱
You have to open the app
When you're at a restaurant or just finished eating, opening an app and searching a database is three steps too many.
🔍
The database is stale
Searching for "Chipotle chicken bowl" returns 47 user-submitted entries with different values. Which one is right? Nobody knows.
⏱️
Logging takes 3 minutes per meal
Three meals plus snacks adds up to 12+ minutes of manual logging per day. That's a habit most people drop within weeks.
😶
One missed day becomes a missed week
Once you stop opening the app daily, the streak breaks and most people never restart. The habit has a fragile foundation.
